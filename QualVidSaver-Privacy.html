隐私权政策

本应用不会收集、存储或共享任何用户的个人信息。

应用仅用于在用户授权的前提下，从网页中提取和下载公开可见的视频内容。

应用不会访问用户的联系人、位置、相机、麦克风等敏感权限。

所有处理过程均在本地完成，应用不会将任何数据上传至服务器。

Privacy Policy

This app does not collect, store, or share any personal information.

The app is solely used to parse and download publicly accessible videos from websites with user consent.

It does not access sensitive permissions such as contacts, location, camera, or microphone.

All processing is done locally on the user's device, and no data is uploaded to any server.
